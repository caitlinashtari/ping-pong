Ping-Pong Time

Want to pong? Here's how it works:

  1. Enter a number. Let's stay postive here.
  2. We'll count up to your number from 1 so you don't have to, unless...
    • Your number is divisible by 3, in which case it will be replaced with the word "ping"... just because.
    • Your number is divisible by 5, in which case it will magically turn into the word "pong"...
    • Your number is divisible by 15, at which point it will morph into the compound word "pingpong" because ping-pong.
  3. Let's get started!
